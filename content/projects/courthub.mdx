## The Problem

Sports facility operators need a scalable platform to onboard venues, manage bookings, and run multi-tenant operations without per-client custom builds.

## What I Built

Contributed full-stack features to CourtHub at Symph — a multi-tenant SaaS sports platform covering facility onboarding, location setup, and core booking workflows.

## How It Works

Next.js powers the frontend. NestJS APIs handle tenant-scoped business logic with PostgreSQL via Supabase. I shipped spreadsheet/manual onboarding improvements, Google Maps location selection, contact validation, and facility settings UX.

## Outcome / Results

Production software with real users. Improved onboarding conversion paths and hardened validation across tenant-aware facility setup flows.
