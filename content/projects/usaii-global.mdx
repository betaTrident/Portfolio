## The Problem

Hackathon teams need to move from unstructured brainstorming to executable plans without losing human oversight over AI-generated proposals.

## What I Built

A human-in-the-loop planning workspace with multi-stage agents, context retrieval, and proposal validation — turning team chats and project artifacts into structured execution plans.

## How It Works

FastAPI powers the agent backend. Context retrieval grounds each planning stage in relevant artifacts. Humans approve or reject agent proposals before plans are finalized. Redis caches session state for responsive iteration during the hackathon.

## Outcome / Results

**Finalist** at the USAII Global AI Hackathon 2026. The system demonstrated practical agentic workflows with clear human checkpoints rather than black-box automation.
