## The Problem

A small coffee shop needed affordable order processing, sales tracking, and inventory management across web and desktop.

## What I Built

A client-based POS system supporting daily retail operations with order processing, sales tracking, and inventory modules.

## How It Works

React frontend with Firebase for real-time data sync. Designed for a small business environment with straightforward sales and stock workflows accessible from web and desktop clients.

## Outcome / Results

Delivered end-to-end from planning through deployment and client support — giving the shop a purpose-built system for daily operations.
