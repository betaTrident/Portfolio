## The Problem

A motorparts and accessories store needed a unified system for inventory monitoring, customer management, and daily point-of-sale operations.

## What I Built

A full-stack retail POS with inventory tracking, customer profiles, transaction processing, receipts, and operational reporting.

## How It Works

React + TypeScript frontend over Django REST APIs. PostgreSQL stores products, stock levels, customers, and sales history. Modules cover stock control, sales workflows, and day-to-day operational visibility.

## Outcome / Results

Streamlined stock control and sales workflows for a live retail business — replacing manual tracking with a single operational system.
