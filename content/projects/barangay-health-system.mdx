## The Problem

Barangay San Roque (Ciudad) health center staff needed a structured system for resident profiling and health center operations.

## What I Built

A cross-platform barangay health center management system with integrated resident profiling for Barangay San Roque, Cebu City.

## How It Works

React + TypeScript frontend with Django backend APIs. PostgreSQL stores resident profiles and health center records. Workflows support profiling, record management, and staff-facing operational tasks.

## Outcome / Results

Replaced fragmented paper and spreadsheet workflows with a centralized system tailored to local barangay health operations.
