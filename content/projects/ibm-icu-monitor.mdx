## The Problem

ICU clinicians need early warning on patient deterioration across multiple vital signals — with concise, actionable briefs rather than raw data overload.

## What I Built

A multi-agent ICU monitoring system that analyzes patient vitals, detects cross-signal conflicts, and generates risk scores plus SBAR briefs for clinical handoffs.

## How It Works

Agent pipelines process time-series vitals with pandas and Polars. watsonx.ai powers clinical reasoning layers. The React dashboard surfaces risk tiers and generated SBAR summaries in real time.

## Outcome / Results

Built during IBM Dev Day: Bob Edition 2026. Demonstrated multi-agent clinical decision support with traceable risk scoring under hackathon constraints.
